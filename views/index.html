Learning arabic ?

There is no need to explain the importance of learning Arabic here. Many of us learn the language at basic level and want to improve upon it to master it. The only way for this is to work hard, have patience and practice , practice, practice. Ma`sha-allah this site has collections of Arabic - English phrases. Each of the below sections provides random 15 phrases from the repository. You can simply go through each and take a test on those phrases. Insha-allah everyday practice for 5-10 mins will give you greater edge on learning. Alhamdulillah you can access this site from your mobile too.
